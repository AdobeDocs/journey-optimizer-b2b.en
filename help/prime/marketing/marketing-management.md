## Journey hub

The journey hub is a centralized, AI-driven starting point for all journey/program-based capabilities across Journey Optimizer B2B Prime. It is designed to accelerate first-time user success while progressively unlocking advanced intelligence, metrics, and control for returning and power users.

The hub acts as:

* A guided starting point for creating journeys, programs, and campaigns

* A visibility layer for journey health, performance, and AI insights

* A control center for arbitration, optimization, and recommendations

### Hub Structure (High-Level)

1. AI Assistant - Prompt-based area and _quick start_
1. Adaptive library panel on the right
   * Personalized library
   * Nudge users to arbiterate and optimize
1. Reports on active journeys 

### Empty and error states

* No journeys - prompt Quick Start

* Low data - explain what is missing

* AI unavailable - graceful fallback with explanation
